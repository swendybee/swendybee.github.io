Teaching a dog to skate board

Put the side of a big cardboard box on four even stacks of books. Lure the dog on with a treat. If it dosent get on right away, practice some more. Then put the skateboard on the sidewalk. Lure the dog on with a treat. Give the skateboard a little push, and we're off!

Fetch

Throw a ball. The dog doesn't know to bring back the ball, so hold up a treat. If it doesen't bring the ball, keep throwing the ball. Soon youll be having a fetchin time!

If you don't want to train it, you can go to a dog trainer.
